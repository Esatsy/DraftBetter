Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

TacticsMaster ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly or that is automatically collected when using TacticsMaster:

  • Riot Account Data: Your summoner name, TFT rank, match history, and gameplay statistics accessed via the Riot Games API
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the application (features used, preferences)
  • Device Information: Operating system version for compatibility purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide personalized TFT recommendations and insights
  • Display your stats, match history, and performance analytics
  • Improve our AI models and recommendation accuracy
  • Fix bugs and enhance the application

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device. We do not store your personal game data on external servers. AI model training uses anonymized, aggregated match data from public Riot API endpoints.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Riot Games API: To fetch your TFT data (subject to Riot's Privacy Notice)

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Your Riot API credentials are never stored or transmitted through our servers.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we have about you
  • Request deletion of your local data
  • Opt out of data collection by uninstalling the application

7. Children's Privacy

TacticsMaster is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
